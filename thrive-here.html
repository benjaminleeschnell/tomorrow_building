THRIVE HERE

Iron sharpens iron.

We're building a community of some of the smartest, most innovative, and optimistic people so that they can in turn develop the most smartest, innovative, best versions of themselves and their ventures.

From intimate fireside chats with startup champions, lunch & learns with thought-leaders to themed pot-luck dinners, our programming will be designed for you to co-learn and thrive both socially and professionally!

But we also know that visionaries desire community as well as autonomy. That's why we're leaving it up to you to decide which events and programs tickle your fancy... and when you need alone time to regroup or rest!
